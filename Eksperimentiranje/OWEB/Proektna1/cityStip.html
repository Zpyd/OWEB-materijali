Isar Fortress

The Isar Fortress is a medieval fortress located in Stip. It offers panoramic views of the city and surrounding areas. The fortress has a rich history dating back to ancient times and is a must-visit for history enthusiasts.

City Center

The heart of Stip features charming streets, local shops, and traditional restaurants. Experience authentic Macedonian culture and hospitality in this vibrant area filled with historical architecture.

Church of St. Nicholas

A beautiful Orthodox church with stunning frescoes and religious art. This historical church is an important cultural landmark and showcases traditional Macedonian religious architecture.

City Park

A peaceful green space perfect for relaxation and leisurely walks. The park features beautiful gardens, walking paths, and is a favorite spot for locals to gather and enjoy nature.
